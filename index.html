Workshop Overview

The Neuromorphic Computing for Development and Learning Workshop (NCDL) is a full-day event dedicated to exploring the latest advancements in neuromorphic sensing, spiking neural networks, developmental learning, and embodied intelligence.

  • Keynote talks from leading researchers
  • Hands-on tutorials on neuromorphic techniques
  • Poster session and industrial showcases
  • Networking and live streaming
