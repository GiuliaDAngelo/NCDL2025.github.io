Neuromorphic Computing for Development and Learning Workshop (NCDL)

Full-Day Workshop - International Conference on Development and Learning (ICDL)

About the Workshop

The Neuromorphic Computing for Development and Learning Workshop (NCDL) is a full-day event focused on advancements in neuromorphic computing and developmental learning. It brings together experts, researchers, and industry professionals to explore neuromorphic sensing, spiking neural networks (SNNs), event-driven computing, and embodied learning systems. Keynote talks and hands-on sessions will cover sensorimotor learning, adaptive perception, real-time decision-making, and neuromorphic architectures.
